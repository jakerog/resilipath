## Executive Summary

Phase 4 transitions ResiliPath from a tactical execution and planning tool into a strategic **Resilience Intelligence** platform. The focus is on automating vendor risk assessments, implementing machine-learning driven resilience scoring, and providing a unified compliance mapper for global regulations.

### Core Objectives
- **Vendor Risk Management (VRM)**: Automated assessments and performance tracking for critical 3rd party providers.
- **Resilience Scoring (ML)**: Predictive analytics to identify weak points in continuity plans.
- **Compliance Mapper**: Unified dashboard for mapping resilience activities to SOC 2, ISO 27001, and DORA.
- **Partner API**: Public-facing REST API for enterprise integration.
