## Summary

Successfully implemented the interactive resilience planning workflow. Tenants can now initialize plans from templates and complete them using a "Guided Interview" wizard that integrates critical data from their Asset Registry.

---

## Work Completed

### 1. Guided Interview Framework
- Developed the `GuidedInterview.tsx` component, providing a section-by-section wizard for BCP data collection.
- Supports varied input types: text areas, single-choice buttons, and specialized asset-linking interfaces.
- Implemented real-time progress tracking and auto-save capabilities.

### 2. Asset Registry Integration
- Deeply integrated the Asset Registry (from Sub-Phase 2.1) into the planning process.
- The `asset-link` question type allows planners to explicitly map critical systems and services to specific sections of their resilience plan, ensuring architectural alignment between execution and planning.

### 3. Plan Management UI
- Enhanced the Plan Gallery with initialization logic.
- Created the dynamic `PlanEditor` route to manage existing plans, ensuring full tenant isolation during the editing process.

---

## Architectural Decisions

### Data Schema Extensibility
- Designed the `BCPTemplate` schema to be highly flexible. Questions can be added or modified without changing the underlying engine logic, allowing for rapid deployment of new regulatory or industry-standard templates.

### Isolated Contextual Fetching
- All assets fetched during the interview process are strictly filtered by the current `tenantId` in custom claims, maintaining the core ResiliPath isolation mandate.
