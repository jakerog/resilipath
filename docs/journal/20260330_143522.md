- ISO 22301-aligned structure with 11 sections
- Annual review requirement (§9.2)
- Crisis Management Team and BCP Team distinction (§3)
- Business Impact Analysis required (§4)
- Contingency planning for high-risk/high-volume products (§5)
- Communication protocols for employees, media, customers, vendors (§6.9)
- Recovery stages: Recovery, Salvage, Return to Normal (§7)
- Training and testing requirements (§8)

#### From DR Runbook Excel
- 90 tasks across 8 stages
- 15+ teams involved (NTT, GPI, TCS, etc.)
- Timing tracking with variance analysis
- Evidence requirements per task
- Go/No-Go decision points (Tasks 17, 67, 82)
- Resource availability is critical issue
- Mock 1, Mock 2, Mock 3, Production phases

#### From DR Hub PDF
- 7 core database tables (Resource, Vendor, Team, Exercise, Stage, Task)
- 4 RBAC roles (Admin, User, Report, Moderator)
- PWA/mobile-native UX requirement
- Skeuomorphic design requirement
- Email engine with lists, templates, scheduling
- 4 required reports (Actual Duration, Actual vs. Estimated, Variance, Stage Completion)
- Resource check-in functionality
