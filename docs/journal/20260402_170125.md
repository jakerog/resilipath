## Summary

Implemented the foundational governance framework for resilience plans. This includes automated tracking of review cycles and a centralized UI for monitoring plan status and upcoming deadlines, ensuring consistent audit readiness across all tenants.

---

## Work Completed

### 1. Governance Schema Extension
- Updated `ResiliencePlan` with `reviewCycleDays` and `nextReviewAt` fields.
- This allows tenants to define custom review intervals (e.g., 180 days for bi-annual, 365 for annual) per plan.

### 2. Automated Lifecycle Triggers
- Developed the `onPlanUpdated` Firestore trigger in `functions/src/planning/triggers.ts`.
- The trigger automatically calculates and sets the `nextReviewAt` date whenever a plan is approved or its review configuration is modified.

### 3. Review Dashboard Widget
- Developed the `ReviewSummary.tsx` component, providing an executive view of plan health.
- Highlights overdue reviews and upcoming deadlines (within 30 days) to proactive risk management.
- Integrated the summary into the central Plans Gallery.

---

## Architectural Decisions

### Event-Driven Governance
- Decided to use Firestore triggers for review date calculation rather than client-side logic. This ensures that the governance clock is always updated consistently, regardless of how the plan status was modified (UI, API, or automated workflow).

### Proactive Awareness
- Implemented the 30-day "Upcoming" window in the UI to give planners sufficient lead time to conduct thorough reviews before they become overdue.
